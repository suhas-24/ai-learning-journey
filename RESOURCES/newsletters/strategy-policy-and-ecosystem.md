# Strategy, Policy, And Ecosystem

This guide covers newsletters that help you understand the broader environment around model capability, risk, governance, and industry direction.

## Import AI

### What it teaches

Import AI helps place technical progress inside a larger strategic context. It is useful when you need to think beyond APIs and tools and ask how capability shifts, policy questions, and ecosystem moves affect engineering choices.

### Major themes and subtopics

- Policy and governance context.
- Frontier model implications.
- Strategic changes in the AI ecosystem.

### Best phases

- Phase 2 for broad context.
- Phase 8 for guardrails and risk framing.

## Why This Category Matters

- Safety decisions are easier to justify when you understand the larger context.
- Portfolio work improves when you can explain why a system matters, not just how it works.
