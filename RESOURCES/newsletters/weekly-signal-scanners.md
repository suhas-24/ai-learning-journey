# Weekly Signal Scanners

This guide covers newsletters that are useful for staying aware of changes in the ecosystem without getting buried in detail.

## The Batch

### What it teaches

The Batch is useful for broad applied-AI awareness. It helps you notice major movements in models, products, and research without needing to monitor dozens of primary sources yourself.

### Major themes and subtopics

- Applied AI trends.
- Research highlights with practical framing.
- Ecosystem shifts that matter to builders.

### Best phases

- Phases 2 through 11 as a light weekly scan.

## Latent Space

### What it teaches

Latent Space is useful for understanding the builder ecosystem around AI engineering. It often connects tools, workflow shifts, and product patterns in a way that helps portfolio-minded builders stay current.

### Major themes and subtopics

- Builder conversations and tool adoption.
- AI product patterns.
- Workflow changes in the ecosystem.

### Best phases

- Phases 4, 7, and 11.

## Ben's Bites

### What it teaches

Ben's Bites is best used as a quick scanner for launches, experiments, and product movement. Its value is speed, not depth.

### Major themes and subtopics

- New tools and launches.
- Builder experimentation patterns.
- Fast ecosystem scanning.

### Best phases

- Phase 2 for awareness.
