# People Reference

People are not here for hero worship. They are here because the best teachers in this space compress years of experience into a few clear patterns, warnings, and mental models.

Use this folder as a second set of explanations. If one person's writing makes a hard idea click, that usually means they found a simpler way to say the same thing.

## How To Learn From A Person

- Notice the ideas they repeat.
- Compare their advice with what you see in your own project.
- Extract principles, not personality.

## Guides In This Folder

- [systems-thinkers.md](./systems-thinkers.md) covers people who explain AI as a system of tradeoffs, feedback loops, and reliability.
- [model-builders-and-research-explainers.md](./model-builders-and-research-explainers.md) covers people who make model internals and research easier to understand.
- [tool-builders-and-practical-operators.md](./tool-builders-and-practical-operators.md) covers people who teach through workflows, tools, and real project decisions.
