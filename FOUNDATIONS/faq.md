# FAQ

## Do I need to finish every chapter before building?

No. The rhythm should be learn a concept, build something small, then come back for the next concept.

## Can I skip straight to agents?

You can, but the result is usually brittle. Without Python, APIs, and debugging habits, later phases feel magical until they break.

## Do I need to know machine learning math deeply?

Not to start. You need enough intuition to understand what models are good at, where they fail, and how retrieval or evaluation changes system quality.

## When should I use AI coding tools?

Use them as accelerators, not replacements for understanding. If they write something you cannot explain, slow down and study it.

## How do I know I am ready for the next phase?

You are ready when you can explain the current phase in simple language and build a small version of the main idea.
